## Full Description

Barbell Labs is the workout tracker built for everyone who trains — gym, home, garage, or anywhere you break a sweat. Whether it's your first session or your thousandth, Barbell Labs grows with you.

No clutter. No confusion. Log your workout and get back to lifting.

---

**THE MORE YOU LOG, THE MORE YOU SEE**
Every session you complete builds your progression story. Dual-line charts show your weight and reps climbing together over time — scrub through your entire history and watch your strength stack up session by session. The longer you use Barbell Labs, the more powerful it becomes.

**AI COACHING, BUILT IN**
After every session, Barbell Labs analyzes your history and tells you exactly what to do next. Coming back after time off? It eases you in. Stalled on the same weight for 3 sessions? It tells you it's time to break through. Leaving reps in the tank? It pushes you harder. Real coaching, based on your real data — no subscription required.

**TRACK EFFORT LIKE A SERIOUS LIFTER**
Log RPE (Rate of Perceived Exertion) and RIR (Reps in Reserve) on every set. Know exactly how hard you worked and how much you had left. The data you collect now becomes the coaching you get tomorrow.

**YOUR PRs, FRONT AND CENTER**
Your Bench, Squat, and Deadlift records sit at the top of your Progress tab. A crown marks your current PR — tracked by both weight and reps at that weight. Chase it every session.

**BUILT TO KEEP YOU CONSISTENT**
Streak tracking keeps the habit alive. Quick-log your last session to repeat a workout in one tap. The rest timer with haptic feedback keeps your training tight between sets.

**SIMPLE ENOUGH FOR DAY ONE**
First time in the gym? Start logging in seconds. No setup, no templates to configure, no learning curve. Just add your exercise, enter your sets, and go.

**YOUR DATA, YOUR WAY**
Cloud sync keeps your workouts across all your devices instantly. Export everything to CSV and open it in Excel or Google Sheets — your data is always yours.
